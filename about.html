    Who are we?

    BAZUZE is an agriculture commodity platform located in Nigeria that allows for
    individual an opportunity to earn from the proceeds of farming. Bazuze does this
    through the empowerment of underserved small farm owners by providing them
    access to funds for farming, training on modern rearing, improved process and by
    creating a ready market for those commodities.

    Why Agriculture

    Why not agriculture? Agriculture contributes about 30% of Africa’s GDP and almost
    70% of employment in Africa. It’s an industry in which its investment keeps pace
    with inflation and increase in value over the long-term.

    One of the major challenges faced by small farmers that is directly affecting their
    income and standard of living is the Access to Markets. Lack of storage facilities
    and access to ready market poses serious threats to farmers, where they are
    unable to sell off their produce at a good price after harvest. Thus leads to massive
    post-harvest losses and food wastage. Bazuze is creating a platform for these and
    we are socially influencing the livelihood, increase the productivity of the partner
    farmers, and create wealth for our financial partners through commodity trading.
